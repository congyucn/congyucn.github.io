Short Biography

Cong Yu received B.S. degree from the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China, Chengdu, China, in 2019. He is currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree at the School of Information and Communication Engineering, University of Electronic Science and Technology of China, Chengdu, China. He is supervised by Prof. Yan Chen and Prof. Yang Hu. His research interests include deep adversarial learning, recommendation system and computer vision.
